About us

We are a team of researchers and engineers who are serious in machine learning and computer vision.

Computing resource

We built Trinity, a GPU cluster shared across three labs in KHU CSE. Trinity is equipped with 16 x 4090, 4 x A6000, 56 x 3090, and 24 x A5000 as of March 2024. We use a resource manager to efficiently use these GPUs. Also, we have access to Seraph, a gigantic GPU cluster shared across three departments in the College of Software, Kyung Hee University. Seraph is equipped with 298 A6000/A5000/3090 GPUs.

How to join our group
